# 🇵🇭 Philippine Energy Consumption Analysis

A data analysis project exploring 20+ years of 
Philippine electricity consumption data (2003–2024).

## Tools Used
- Python, Pandas, Matplotlib

## Data Source
Department of Energy Philippines — doe.gov.ph

## Key Findings
- Average annual growth rate of 4.3%
- Luzon dominates with 90,269 GWh in 2024
- Peak consumption occurs in May (summer season)
- Coal accounts for 62.5% of energy mix in 2024
